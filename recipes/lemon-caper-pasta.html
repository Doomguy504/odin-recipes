Lemon Caper Pasta

Credit: Allrecipes

Description

Lemon caper pasta is a light and refreshing dish that combines the tangy flavors of lemon and capers with pasta. It is often served as a main course or side dish, and can be made with various types of pasta such as spaghetti, linguine, or penne. The dish is typically garnished with fresh herbs like parsley or basil.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti or linguine
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice and zest
  • Caper berries
  • Fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  3. Add lemon juice, zest, and capers to the pan.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta in the sauce and garnish with parsley.
